How It All Began

It all started as a simple Valentine’s Day date with my Fiance. We wanted to do something different so we frequented the Groupon app and found a nice little deal for a local brewery a few towns over. Thinking that the place would be full of love birds we reserved table to ensure that we would have a nice little intimate spot to savour our experience.

Off we go to Shebeen Brewing Company We were so intrigued to see all the different selections of beers and the amount of different styles. For one not knowing anything about beer you don’t understand the various styles and flavors they come in. Like most basic uneducated beer drinker all you think of when you think of beer is you basic mass produced brands.

So many options and styles are beers to choose I honestly I never knew the difference between an Ale, stout. For me beer has always been just beer; someone gives you a beer you don’t ask questions you drink it even if you have to grimace through it.

Now my curiousity and thirst for exploration leads me to this social media and hoppy quest to expand my palate and cure my ignorance. Since women aren’t usually seen as heavy beer drinkers I thought I would name my page to represent the unrepresented, which led me to the name, “Women Love Beer Too.”

I want to do this page all in good fun and not make it seem like work. I want to document my experiences, opinions, and even create some relationship memories. Not sure how far this would go or if I would have people on here who care for me and my soapbox, but screw it. This venture like beer drinking is all in good fun.
